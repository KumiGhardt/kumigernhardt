YWCA of Malawi

I began working with the Young Women's Christian Association (YWCA) of Malawi as a teen and continued on as Communications officer and Project manager, collaboration with the World YWCA to bring much needed assiatnce to women in girls in rural Malawi, Southern African and even on the world stage in all issues that affect women from literacy, sexual reproductive health and rights to business and spiritual matters. In this position, I achieved the following:

  • Lobbied for funding for programs for the local young women in the 4 YWCA Malawi branches resulting in increased youth participation in economic and educational programs.
  • Managed projects that worked with women and girls at the grassroots that resulted in the expansion of access to evidence-based comprehensive sexuality and reproductive health education and service referrals among young people.
  • Attended International conferences in Mexico and Austria to raise awareness about Malawian issues that resulted in increased funding. Streamlined communication between the main YWCA Malawi office and its branches.
  • Conceived the online presence both on Facebook and the YWCA Mw website to bring a global awareness of the work of YWCA Malawi.
  • Established affiliations with organisations such as Girls not Brides, World YWCA and other domestic organisations to share resources and key findings in research.

Journalism

I worked as a journalist for 5 years for the Malawi Broadcasting Corporation as a Producer, Report and Editor for their Radio, TV and online presences during which I:

  • Oversaw the production of the main news broadcast in TV and Radio resulting in a smooth news broadcast news and the rearrangement of new items should breaking news be needed to be given priority for the benefit of the viewer and the news anchor.
  • Conducted the editing of new scripts and other news-based programs to ensure news items were factual and grammatical.
  • Reported for the public broadcaster with emphasis on political and women's rights issues to bring greater awareness to the public. Accomplished the implementation of the launch of the MBC online publication and introduced BBC news reporting techniques
During my Masters degree studies in Global Communication and International Journalism, I interned at Reset.org writing blogs with an emphasis on smart approaches to sustainability resulting in an output of two blog posts a week
